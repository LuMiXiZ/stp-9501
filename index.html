Pizza Rush

The Ultimate Pizza Delivery Challenge

Step into the heat of the kitchen and prove you can handle the rush!
In Pizza Rush, you'll take on the role of a high-speed pizza chef,battling time and hunger to deliver fresh, hot pies to impatient customers.
Are you fast enough to keep up?

Welcome to Pizza Rush!

Get ready for a chaotic yet thrilling experience where timing, strategy, and quick thinking make all the difference. Pizza Rush blends restaurant management with real-time action as you serve, cook, and upgrade your way to pizza stardom. From self-service stations to dough machines, every second counts.

Key Features

Fast-Paced Arcade Action:

Collect, cook, and serve under pressure.

Self-Service Chaos:

Let customers make their own pizzas in an unpredictable twist.

Upgrade
System:

Use your hard-earned tips to improve speed, machines, and customer flow.

Endless Orders:

Can you survive the waves of pizza-hungry customers?

Easy to Learn, Hard to Master:

Simple controls with deep strategic options.

How to Play

1

Collect dough from customers and place it in the machine to start pizza production.

2

Move fast — customers won't wait forever!

3

Guide them to the self-service room for creative, hands-on pizza making.

4

Earn tips, avoid chaos, and upgrade your kitchen gear.

5

Stay calm and serve hot — it’s the only way to win the Pizza Rush.
